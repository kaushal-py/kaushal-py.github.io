About me


I like computers a lot. Right from my childhood, video games interested me. Not only because they were fun to play, but also because I was always curious about how these games were made. In the process of trying to learn to make video games, I got introduced to programming. I think programming made me very powerful. I started imagining the computer as a "mini universe" and I was it's God. I coud write any sorts of rules, run all kinds of simulations, and the computer would do so on my command.


With this, I decided to pursue my Bachelors in Information Technology. During this period, I got to know the importance of mathematics in this field. It was very exciting to apply mathematical concepts (which I earlier thought were purely theoretical) into some of the applications I worked on. Also, I got interested in certain research domains, and aiming to get a Masters degree soon.


As I said, programming did make me feel very powerful. Being so much empowered by it gave me a lot of happiness. And this made me grow my interest in this field. But, as Spiderman always says "With great power comes great responsibility". I want to use my abilites to work for the future of India.


On the lighter side about me, I like wactching vidoes about music, space and the future. And I do play a lot of video games in my free time.
